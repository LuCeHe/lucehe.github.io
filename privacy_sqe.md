SQE Mastery ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy.
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information
when you use our app to practise multiple-choice questions and questionnaires.
By using SQE Mastery, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

SQE Mastery is designed as a questionnaire and learning app and collects minimal data.

1.1 Information You Provide
- Account Information (if applicable): such as an email address or username,
  if you choose to create an account.
- User Inputs: your answers to questions, quiz progress, scores, and preferences
  within the app.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information
- App Usage Information: interactions with questions, features used, and session duration.
- Device Information: device type, operating system version, and app version.
- Technical Data: anonymous logs and diagnostics used to improve app performance
  and stability.

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as identity documents,
payment details, or precise location data.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:
- To provide access to questionnaires and learning features
- To track progress and display results
- To improve app functionality and question quality
- To respond to support requests if you contact us

We do not sell your personal data.
